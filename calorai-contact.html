Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with CalorAI?

Download CalorAI from the Google Play Store, create your profile with your goals, and start logging your meals using any of our 6 convenient methods!

Is CalorAI free?

CalorAI offers a generous free tier with all essential features. Premium plans unlock advanced analytics, unlimited AI scans, and more detailed nutrient tracking.

How accurate is the AI food recognition?

Our AI has been trained on millions of food images and achieves over 90% accuracy. You can always review and adjust portion sizes before logging.

Do you offer enterprise solutions?

Yes! We provide custom enterprise packages for gyms, nutritionists, healthcare providers, and corporate wellness programs. Contact us for more details.
