Realify

Child Safety

Overview

Realify is a comprehensive cross-platform solution designed to detect and label AI-generated videos, helping parents and educators protect children from synthetic content that may confuse their understanding of reality. Research shows children under 8 cannot reliably distinguish AI-generated content from real videos, making this protection critical in the age of photorealistic AI video generation.

Key Features

  • ML Detection: Advanced machine learning models automatically analyze videos in real-time
  • Metadata Verification: Check for C2PA and SynthID watermarks confirming content authenticity
  • Community Validation: Trust-weighted crowdsourcing for improved accuracy
  • Parental Controls: Customizable age-appropriate filtering with sensitivity levels
  • Cross-Platform: Works on YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, Facebook via browser extension and mobile apps
