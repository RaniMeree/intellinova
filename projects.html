CalorAI

Health & Nutrition

Overview

CalorAI is an advanced AI-powered nutrition and meal planning platform designed to revolutionize how people approach their health and dietary goals. With over 50,000 downloads and 500,000+ meals logged, CalorAI provides personalized meal recommendations, intelligent calorie tracking, and comprehensive nutritional insights.

Key Features

  • AI-Powered Food Tracking: Log meals in 6 ways - photo, video, voice, text, and more
  • Smart Calorie Counter: Automated food recognition using computer vision
  • Comprehensive Tracking: Track vitamins, minerals, and 30+ nutrients automatically
  • AI Nutrition Assistant: Chat for instant tips on calories, macros, and healthy choices
  • Social Hub & Gamification: Share meals, compete on leaderboards, and level up

Realify

Child Safety

Overview

Realify is a comprehensive cross-platform solution designed to detect and label AI-generated videos, helping parents and educators protect children from synthetic content. Research shows children under 8 cannot reliably distinguish AI-generated content.

Key Features

  • ML Detection: Advanced ML models analyze videos in real-time
  • Metadata Verification: Check for C2PA and SynthID watermarks
  • Community Validation: Trust-weighted crowdsourcing for accuracy
  • Parental Controls: Customizable age-appropriate filtering
  • Cross-Platform: Works on YouTube, TikTok, Instagram via browser extension
