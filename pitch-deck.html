AI is ready.
Adoption is not.

Realify
Reality Check for the Internet
We live in a world full of AI-generated content. It affects how we perceive reality and more importantly, how our children do. Realify detects what is real and what is AI-generated, then acts on that distinction for different audiences.
A simple label, AI or real, with a confidence score. For children, filtered protection before they ever see it. For public figures, an authenticity stamp only they can add.
🔭 Current stage: R&D and concept validation. Intellivation's next major owned product after CalorAI.
AI Detection
Computer Vision
Deepfake Detection
Content Safety
R&D
Three-Audience Solution
👁️ For Adults

A simple AI or real label with a confidence score on every video or image. Full transparency so you always know what you are actually watching.

👧 For Children

Flagged AI content gets filtered before children see it. Kids should not have to question whether what they are watching is even real. That is our job.

⭐ For Celebrities and Public Figures

An electronic authenticity stamp, only the real person can add it. When you see a public figure online, you can instantly verify if they approved it.
